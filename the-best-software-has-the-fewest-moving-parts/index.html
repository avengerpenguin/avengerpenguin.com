The best software has the fewest moving parts

Dave Farley has said at various times "the ability to change your software is the is the defining characteristic of its quality". This comes up for example in The Ultimate Quality Of GOOD Software.

Combining this idea with a sprinkling of Occam's razor we get a principle that we should largely favour less in software engineering wherever possible.
